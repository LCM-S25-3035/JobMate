# User Roles & Personas

## 1. Applicant

- **Description**: Job seekers in Ontario’s tech sector, primarily targeting roles in data science, machine learning, data engineering, analytics, and AI.
- **Goals**:

  - Discover job listings that align with their skills and experience.
  - Customize resumes for each application to increase ATS compatibility.
  - Apply efficiently to multiple roles via automation.

- **Skill Level**: Intermediate to advanced (technically proficient, but not necessarily in job search optimization).
- **Pain Points**:

  - Difficulty tailoring resumes for every application.
  - Low visibility in ATS due to poor keyword alignment.
  - Time-intensive manual job search and application process.

## 2. Recruiter

- **Description**: HR professionals or hiring managers seeking candidates for tech/data roles within competitive Ontario markets.
- **Goals**:

  - Source qualified applicants with relevant technical skills.
  - Reduce time spent filtering unqualified or mismatched resumes.
  - Improve quality of candidates progressing through the funnel.

- **Skill Level**: Intermediate; familiar with ATS systems and keyword-based filtering.
- **Pain Points**:

  - High volume of irrelevant applications.
  - Resumes that are difficult to assess due to inconsistent formatting.
  - Time-consuming manual evaluation of resumes lacking contextual fit.
