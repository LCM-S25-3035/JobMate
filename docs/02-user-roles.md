# User Roles & Personas

## Overview

JobMate serves two distinct user types with specialized interfaces and workflows. Each role has a dedicated theme and user experience designed to optimize their specific needs in the job market ecosystem.

---

## 1. Applicant (Job Seeker)

### Profile
- **Primary Users**: Tech professionals in Ontario seeking opportunities in data science, machine learning, software development, data engineering, analytics, and AI
- **Demographics**: 
  - Age: 22-45 years
  - Education: Bachelor's to PhD in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, or related fields
  - Experience: Entry-level to senior positions (0-15+ years)
- **Tech Savviness**: Intermediate to advanced technical skills, varying job search optimization knowledge

### User Interface Theme
- **Color Scheme**: Lilac/Purple gradient (#9d4edd, #c77dff, #7b2cbf)
- **Design Philosophy**: Inspirational, growth-oriented, and empowering
- **Visual Elements**: Soft gradients, encouraging imagery, progress indicators

### Goals & Objectives
**Primary Goals:**
- Discover relevant job opportunities matching their technical expertise
- Create and optimize resumes for ATS compatibility
- Apply efficiently to multiple positions with tailored applications
- Track application status and receive feedback
- Build a professional network within the tech community

**Secondary Goals:**
- Improve interview skills through practice and feedback
- Stay updated on industry trends and required skills
- Access career development resources and learning opportunities

### Key Features & Workflows
1. **Job Discovery**:
   - Advanced search with technical skill filters
   - AI-powered job recommendations based on profile
   - Saved searches and job alerts
   - Company research and insights

2. **Application Management**:
   - Resume builder with ATS optimization
   - Cover letter templates and customization
   - Application tracking dashboard
   - Interview scheduling and preparation tools

3. **Profile & Portfolio**:
   - Skills assessment and verification
   - Project showcase and portfolio hosting
   - Professional networking features
   - Achievement and certification tracking

### Pain Points & Challenges
**Current Challenges:**
- **ATS Compatibility**: Resumes often fail to pass Applicant Tracking Systems due to poor formatting or keyword misalignment
- **Volume vs. Quality**: Difficulty balancing quantity of applications with quality customization
- **Market Visibility**: Low response rates despite relevant qualifications
- **Skill Gaps**: Uncertainty about which skills to develop for target roles

**JobMate Solutions:**
- AI-powered resume optimization for ATS compatibility
- Automated application workflows with personalization
- Analytics on application performance and market trends
- Skill gap analysis and learning recommendations

### User Journey
1. **Onboarding**: Profile creation, skill assessment, preference setting
2. **Discovery**: Job searching, saving favorites, setting alerts
3. **Application**: Resume customization, application submission, tracking
4. **Follow-up**: Interview preparation, feedback analysis, continuous improvement

## 2. Recruiter (Hiring Manager)

### Profile
- **Primary Users**: HR professionals, talent acquisition specialists, hiring managers, and technical leads in Ontario's tech sector
- **Demographics**:
  - Age: 28-55 years
  - Role: HR generalists to specialized tech recruiters
  - Company Size: Startups to enterprise organizations
- **Tech Savviness**: Intermediate; proficient with ATS systems, Boolean search, and recruitment tools

### User Interface Theme
- **Color Scheme**: Professional Blue gradient (#0d6efd, #6ea8fe, #0842a0)
- **Design Philosophy**: Efficient, data-driven, and professional
- **Visual Elements**: Clean layouts, data visualizations, action-oriented design

### Goals & Objectives
**Primary Goals:**
- Source and identify qualified candidates efficiently
- Reduce time-to-hire while maintaining quality standards
- Improve candidate experience and company brand
- Build and maintain talent pipelines for future needs

**Secondary Goals:**
- Analyze recruitment metrics and optimize processes
- Collaborate effectively with hiring teams
- Stay competitive in talent acquisition market
- Reduce recruitment costs and improve ROI

### Key Features & Workflows
1. **Job Management**:
   - Job posting creation with AI-powered optimization
   - Multi-platform distribution and syndication
   - Performance analytics and optimization recommendations
   - Collaborative hiring team workflows

2. **Candidate Sourcing**:
   - Advanced candidate search and filtering
   - AI-powered candidate matching and ranking
   - Talent pool management and segmentation
   - Passive candidate engagement tools

3. **Application Processing**:
   - Automated screening and scoring
   - Interview scheduling and coordination
   - Candidate communication templates
   - Feedback collection and analysis

4. **Analytics & Reporting**:
   - Recruitment funnel analytics
   - Time-to-hire and cost-per-hire metrics
   - Candidate source effectiveness
   - Diversity and inclusion reporting

### Pain Points & Challenges
**Current Challenges:**
- **Volume Overload**: High volume of applications with many unqualified candidates
- **Quality Assessment**: Difficulty quickly identifying top candidates from large applicant pools
- **Time Constraints**: Pressure to fill positions quickly while maintaining quality standards
- **Candidate Experience**: Balancing thorough evaluation with timely communication

**JobMate Solutions:**
- AI-powered candidate screening and ranking
- Automated initial communications and status updates
- Comprehensive analytics for process optimization
- Streamlined collaboration tools for hiring teams

### User Journey
1. **Job Creation**: Define requirements, create optimized job postings
2. **Sourcing**: Active search, pipeline management, candidate engagement
3. **Evaluation**: Application review, screening, interview coordination
4. **Decision**: Candidate comparison, team collaboration, offer management
5. **Analysis**: Performance review, process optimization, continuous improvement

## Design Considerations

### Applicant Experience (Lilac Theme)
- **Emotional Design**: Encouraging and aspirational
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Guided workflows with clear next steps
- **Personalization**: Customized recommendations and insights
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for job searching on-the-go

### Recruiter Experience (Blue Theme)
- **Efficiency Focus**: Quick access to key information and actions
- **Data Visualization**: Clear metrics and performance indicators
- **Bulk Operations**: Tools for managing multiple candidates and jobs
- **Collaboration**: Team-based workflows and communication tools

### Recruiter Success Indicators
