# Project Overview

## Project Name

**JobMate**

## Introduction

Job seekers frequently encounter challenges in securing employment opportunities that align with their skills and experiences. With increasing reliance on Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) by employers, many qualified candidates are filtered out due to keyword mismatches or formatting issues. The problem is particularly acute in competitive job markets like Ontario, Canada.

## Job Market Challenges

### Global Challenges

- **Volume of Applications**: Employers receive hundreds of applications per job posting.
- **ATS Limitations**: Over 75% of recruiters use ATS, which often filter resumes lacking exact keyword matches.
- **Time Constraints**: Job seekers spend 11+ hours per week applying for jobs.
- **Skills Gap**: Mismatch between job requirements and applicant qualifications.

### Ontario-Specific Challenges

- **High Competition**: Despite low unemployment, competition remains fierce.
- **Localized Skill Demand**: Strong demand for data, AI, and tech skills.
- **High Rejection Rates**: 60%+ of applicants report rejections without feedback.

## Problem Analysis

| Problem                         | Cause                             | Impact                            | Solution                             |
| ------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Difficulty identifying job fits | Poor job matching systems         | Manual, time-consuming job search | AI-powered job recommendation engine |
| Resume rejections by ATS        | Missing keywords, poor formatting | Reduced interview chances         | NLP-based resume customization       |
| Manual application processes    | Lack of automation tools          | Inefficient job search            | Integrated auto-apply functionality  |

## Proposed Solution

Develop a **Job Recommendation and Resume Customization System** using NLP and ML to:

- Analyze resumes and recommend relevant job listings.
- Tailor resumes for higher ATS compatibility.
- Automate job applications through an integrated platform.

## Target Users

Professionals in Ontario’s tech sector, especially in:

- Data Science
- Machine Learning
- AI-related roles

These users often struggle with resume tailoring and job matching despite high demand for their skills.
